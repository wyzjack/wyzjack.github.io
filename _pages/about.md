I am an incoming Research Scientist/Engineer at Adobe, working on Photoshop. I am a 6th-year Ph.D. candidate in Computer Engineering at Northeastern Unversity, supervised by Prof. [Yun Raymond Fu](http://www1.ece.neu.edu/~yunfu/).
